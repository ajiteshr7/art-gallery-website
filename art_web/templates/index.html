About This Site

ALM Art Gallery is an open source website containing over 100 different varieties of paintings and sculptures, including collections of visuals, performing, and media arts of modern and medieval times. A reflection of our multidisciplinary mission and the increasingly cross-disciplinary practice of artists, ALM gallery reflect a diverse range of artistic pursuits, including: a permanent collection of visual arts, the Moving Image Collection, performing arts commissions, the library’s Collection of artists’ books, Company Collection like those from Pixar, and the Digital Arts Study Collection. Browse our holdings and learn more about the art and artists who animate our collections.
